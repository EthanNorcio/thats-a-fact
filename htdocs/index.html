That's A Fact!

Welcome to this website!
Learn how to fact-check the information you receive.

There is a deadly pandemic, the CoViD-19, disturbing our society right now, and we need reliable information more than ever.
Fact-checking the information we receive is very essential so that we can make correct decisions in this time of uncertainty.

Most people are now tuning in to social media platforms to get information; however, the big problem is that fake news spread like wildfire in these channels.
Aside from internt trolls, some gullible users just believe and share content without actually checking its crediility.

This is the purpose of creating this website: to combat fake news and to let people know which information is a FACT.
